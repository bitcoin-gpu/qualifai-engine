# QualifAI Engine

**The growth engine for mortgage brokers** — AI-driven lead generation, automated follow-up, and
pre-qualification that fills a broker's pipeline with booked, qualified appointments.

> Separate business line from the LOS. **QualifAI Lender** closes loans; **QualifAI Engine** fills the
> pipeline that feeds them. This folder is self-contained — the LOS structure, files, and plan stay
> untouched.

---

## What it is

A done-for-you demand engine for independent mortgage brokers / small IMBs:

```
Lead generation (ads)  →  Automated follow-up  →  AI pre-qualification  →  Booked appointments
```

The broker gets qualified borrowers on their calendar without doing the marketing, chasing, or
manual qualifying themselves. The output (qualified, ready borrowers) is also the perfect on-ramp
to **QualifAI Lender** — the system that takes those deals from application to close.

## Who it's for

Same ICP as the LOS: **broker-owners, principals, founders, presidents** of independent brokerages
(2–15 staff). The hook is easier than the LOS because it's **additive** (no system to switch) and
tied directly to their #1 concern: **more closed loans.**

## Build sequence (deliverable-first)

**Phase 1 — Now:** Sell the half we can deliver → **follow-up + pre-qualification on the broker's
existing / dead lead database** ("reactivation"). Lands a paying client, proves the back half,
generates cash, builds the relationship. Zero ad risk.

**Phase 2 — Parallel:** Find + **pilot-test a mortgage media buyer** on a small budget before
promising ad-generated leads to anyone.

**Phase 3 — Once ads are proven:** Sell the **full engine** (ads + follow-up + pre-qual) as the
premium offer ($3–5k/mo) to new brokers and upsell Phase-1 clients.

**Phase 4 — The prize:** Brokers running the full engine have warm, qualified deals flowing →
"who's processing these?" → upsell **QualifAI Lender (the LOS).**

## ⚠️ Compliance guardrails (build these in from day one)

- **Email reactivation:** OK under **CAN-SPAM** — accurate headers, physical address, working
  unsubscribe, honor opt-outs. **Build the offer email-first.**
- **SMS:** **TCPA requires prior express written consent** — "we have a relationship" is NOT a blanket
  exemption. Penalties $500–1,500/text + class-action risk. Only text contacts with **documented
  consent** (most mortgage lead forms capture it — verify, don't assume). Register **A2P 10DLC**.
  Opt-out ("reply STOP") in every message. Consider an email re-permission step first.
- **Mortgage ad compliance (Phase 3):** Facebook "Credit" Special Ad Category (ECOA); NMLS in ads;
  trigger-term disclosures; state rules.
- **Client agreement:** broker **warrants they have consent** for the contacts they provide.
- **Get a TCPA-savvy attorney** to bless the SMS workflow before scaling.
