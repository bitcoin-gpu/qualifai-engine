# QualifAI Engine — Phase 1: Database Reactivation Offer

The wedge offer. Deliverable **today** with what you already have (GHL + follow-up + pre-qual
automations). Zero ad risk, fast proof, easy first "yes," and a clean on-ramp to the full Engine and
the LOS.

> **The one-liner:** *"I turn the leads you already paid for and gave up on into booked, pre-qualified

---

## Why this offer wins the door

Every broker has a CRM full of **dead leads** — people who inquired months/years ago, weren't ready,
never got worked, and now rot. They already **paid** to acquire them. Most brokers follow up 1–2 times
and quit. **That's free money sitting in their database.**

- **You can deliver it today** — no ads, no creatives, no media buyer needed
- **It's additive** — touches nothing they use now (no "switch systems" objection)
- **It produces the one thing they care about** — booked appointments = deals
- **Proof in days, not months** — appointments show up week one
- **Risk-reversed** — they pay on results, so "yes" is easy
- **It wedges forward** — booked deals → "who's processing these?" → QualifAI Lender (LOS)

---

## What we deliver (the service)

1. **Reactivation campaign** — AI-driven **email-first** re-engagement to the dead list (CAN-SPAM
   compliant), plus **SMS to contacts with documented consent** (opt-out in every message).
2. **AI pre-qualification** — automated qualifying conversation (loan type, timeline, credit ballpark,
   property, income signal) that filters tire-kickers from real borrowers.
3. **Appointment booking** — live, qualified borrowers booked straight onto the broker's calendar.
4. **Long-term nurture** — the not-ready-yet leads drop into an automated nurture so they re-surface
   later (no lead wasted).
5. **Reporting** — # contacted, # re-engaged, # qualified, # appointments booked.

## Objection handling

**"My leads are too old / dead."**
> "That's exactly the point — old leads are free money you already paid for. People's situations
> change; someone not ready 8 months ago might be ready now. The AI finds them so you don't have to
> dig. Worst case, the truly dead ones stay dead and you've lost nothing."

**"I don't want to annoy my database / hurt my reputation."**
> "Totally fair — that's why it's email-first, professional, and anyone who's not interested opts out
> instantly. It's re-engagement, not spam. Done right it *recovers* relationships, doesn't burn them."

**"Isn't texting old leads a compliance problem?"**
> "Good question — it is if you do it wrong. That's why we lead with email (CAN-SPAM compliant) and
> only text contacts who gave consent, which most mortgage lead forms capture. Opt-out in every
> message. We build the compliance in — you stay protected." *(See agreement below.)*

**"What does it cost?"**
> "For the pilot, you only pay when it books a qualified appointment. No appointments, basically no
> cost. I take the risk because I'm confident in what's sitting in your database."

**"I already have someone doing my marketing."**
> "Perfect — this isn't your marketing, it's your *old* leads, which most marketing ignores. It runs
> alongside whatever you've got. Pure upside on assets you already own."

## Client agreement — consent & terms (plain-language skeleton)

> ⚠️ **Not legal advice — have a TCPA-savvy attorney review before use.** This is a starting skeleton.

**1. Service.** QualifAI Engine provides automated re-engagement, pre-qualification, and appointment-
booking technology applied to contact lists the Client provides.

**2. Consent warranty (the critical clause).** Client represents and warrants that it has obtained all
legally required consents to contact each individual on the list it provides, including any prior
express written consent required under the TCPA for SMS/automated messaging, and that no contact is on
a do-not-contact or opt-out list. Client is solely responsible for the lawfulness of its contact data.

**3. Compliance.** Email is sent in compliance with CAN-SPAM (identification, physical address,
functioning opt-out). SMS is sent only to contacts Client has confirmed have provided consent, with
opt-out ("reply STOP") honored. Client maintains records of consent.

**4. Indemnification.** Client indemnifies QualifAI Engine against claims arising from the Client's
contact data, lack of consent, or Client's representations being inaccurate.

**5. Pilot term & fees.** 30-day pilot. Fees as agreed: [setup] + [$ per booked qualified appointment].
"Qualified appointment" = [define: e.g., a contacted lead who confirms intent + a booked calendar slot].

**6. Data.** Client owns its contact data. QualifAI Engine uses it only to deliver the Service.

---

## Definition: "qualified appointment" (agree up front — avoid disputes)

A **qualified appointment** = a contact who (a) responds and confirms genuine interest in a mortgage,
(b) meets the broker's basic pre-qual criteria (loan type + timeline + rough credit/income signal),
and (c) books a time on the broker's calendar. **Define this in writing in every agreement** — it's
what you get paid on.
