## The big strategic decision: ONE motion, not two

Two businesses, one founder, zero customers → **the risk is split focus.** So we merge them:

- **Lead with QualifAI Engine** (dead-lead reactivation) — easy "yes," deliverable today, tied to the
  broker's #1 concern (more deals).
- **QualifAI Lender (LOS) is the upsell** — once they trust you and have warm deals flowing → "who's
  processing all these?" → the LOS conversation.
- **Same 17 brokers, one message, one list.** Not two campaigns. One door, opened with the easier offer.

```
Engine (get borrowers, easy yes)  ──►  trust + warm deal flow  ──►  LOS (close loans, the big sale)
```
